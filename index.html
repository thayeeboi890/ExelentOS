home

ExelentOS is an arch based distro made to bridge windows and other linux users into the arch world. this new-born operating system is the result of combining one of the best desktop environments with top-of-the-line tools for everyday use. the main reason exelentos was created was to create a balance between looks and resources, so you can worry less about memory while having a gorgeus setup with limitless choices!

web browsing

ExelentOS uses Floorp, a fork of firefox that is made to speed up productivity and customization.

office work

ExelentOS uses the libreoffice suite for all needs. from text processing to spreadsheets, it is a open source alternative to ms office.

multimedia

ExelentOS comes preinstalled with a video and music player, a image viewer with 2 editors, and a pdf viewer to suit multimedia needs.

editing

ExelentOS comes preinstalled with krite text editor, simple and effective for those looking for an easy and quick solution. for those who want more... complicated editors, vim is included to make your life... better. gimp and inkscape have also been included for photo editing in both bitmap and vector formats.

software management

ExelentOS now includes discover, a flatpak manager made by kde. it can help you install apps in a breeze. yay is also included for aur use (use at your own risk)

Desktop

ExelentOS comes shipped with the latest version of plasma. It also comes shipped with curated themes to feel soft but also look good. Since exelentos comes with plasma, you can expect easy installation of applications and extensions to fit your need.
