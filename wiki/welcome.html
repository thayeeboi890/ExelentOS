‘ExelentOS’ is a very user friendly OS targeted for beginers.
it has a very human design made for anyone comming from windows.
The caveat about ExelentOS is that it is based on archlinux and is a great start for those that are learning archlinux.
This distro follows the FOSS philosophy and is free for anyone to contribute
